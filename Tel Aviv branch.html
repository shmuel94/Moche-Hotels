Hotel information

The Moche hotel attractive location makes it easy to enjoy downtime at the beach,
a mere two-minute walk away, as well as the many exciting entertainment destinations, tempting restaurants,
and vibrant Tel Aviv night life. Points of interest surround you from all sides: the bustling promenade,
beautiful Frishman and Gordon beaches, famous Carmel Market, HaBima Theater, Dizengoff Center, and more.
Mocha tel aviv Hotel offers an outdoor pool, spa facilities and free Wi-Fi in all areas.

All rooms are air conditioned and feature a minibar, cable TV and free coffee and tea.

Adam & Eve Restaurant serves a varied breakfast that includes a
salad bar and a selection of cheeses and breads. For lunch and
dinner, unique meat dishes are offered. The lobby restaurant
specializes in homemade cakes, alongside salads and sandwiches.

The spa center includes a sauna, a fitness center and rooms for
massage and beauty treatments. The hotel's entertainment staff
organizes cocktail parties, shows, and games.

Price per night Starting from NIS 2000
